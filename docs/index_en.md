# Contextual Regime Indicator (CRI) — Documentation Hub

Welcome to the official documentation of the **Contextual Regime Indicator (CRI)**, a quantitative indicator designed to evaluate the current health of a financial asset by combining two complementary dimensions: stability (Confidence) and risk (Risk).

This site brings together all technical, conceptual, and practical information about CRI V7‑3.
