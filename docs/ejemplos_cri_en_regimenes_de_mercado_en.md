Clear directional movement, low volatility, high coherence.  
Healthy regime.

## Conclusion

These examples show how the CRI clearly and quantitatively captures different market regimes, providing an immediate reading of an asset’s health.  
Its consistent behavior across stable, sideways, volatile, bearish, and shock scenarios reinforces its usefulness as a diagnostic tool for quantitative analysis and decision‑making.
